The Pedalboard

10 SLOTS · ANY ORDER

A board that reorders itself.

Ten slots, any order — drag a block, rewire the rig. Every pedal hand-drawn, photoreal, built from scratch.

01

Drive

DUAL CAPTURE

Two capture slots — a transparent overdrive and a mid-hump boost out of the box. Drop your own capture profiles straight in.

02

Chorus

BBD ANALOG · MONO-SAFE STEREO

One chorus, modelled deep — a triangle LFO swings the bucket-brigade clock rather than the delay time, so the sweep leans the way real analog does, with companding and a touch of drift riding along. Mono-safe, stereo-wide, thick as paint.

03

Delay

TAPE-WARM ECHO · A/B ENGINES

Dual engines in series or parallel, tempo-synced, with a GRIT knob that drives the repeats into beautiful decay — plus wet-only HP/LP to EQ the echoes, and a live tempo lamp whose two LEDs pulse out each engine's echo pattern.

04

Reverb

ONE LUSH SPACE · 3-D

Early reflections into a 16-line modulated hall — three-dimensional, enveloping tails, a shimmer that actually blooms, opt-in ducking, and wet-only HP/LP to shape the tail.

05

Sauce

SPECTRAL FINALIZER

Tone shaping on steroids, with a live before/after spectrum analyser: a synthesized sub-harmonic for real low-end weight, a threshold-tracked high-end de-esser, transient PUNCH and a dynamic 3 kHz de-harsh — watch every push and cut in real time.

Specifications

44.1–96 kHz · MACOS 11+
Formats
VST3, Audio Unit and standalone. macOS 11+, Intel and Apple Silicon.
Chain
Ten slots, any order — gate, comp, drive, amp, cab, sauce, studio, chorus, delay, reverb.
Amps
Three heads, nine voices, eleven captures — level-matched by construction, so switching never jumps your level.
Sample rates
44.1, 48, 88.2 and 96 kHz. Captures resample to their trained rate; every effect follows the host. No 48k lock-in.
Cab
Auto-sense — full-rig captures bypass it, amp-only captures pull the right IR. Or drop in any IR of your own.
Gate
Phase-locked 50/60 Hz harmonic tracking kills single-coil buzz without eating your tails.
Tuner
Strobe-accurate to a hundredth of a cent, and it tracks a soft note through hiss.
Presets
Twenty in the box, and every preset is a JSON file — the folder is the menu.
CPU
One QUALITY knob trades detail against CPU — full fidelity in the studio, featherweight on stage.
