Note detection — here now

Full pYIN: beta-prior thresholding, Viterbi decoding and phase-based refinement hold steady tones inside 1.5 cents and an 80–880 Hz sweep under 5 — verified by a 25-test battery on synthetic and real vocals.

One-knob Auto-Tune — here now

Analyses every note and applies strength-scaled fixes: scale snap, drift removal, over-the-top vibrato taming and plateau recutting — up to fully locked hard-tune at 100%. Snap All pins the whole take to your key.

Eight tools — here now

Main, pitch, vibrato, drift, formant, level, time and cut — drag a blob, flatten a wobble, split a note, snap to scale. Undo everything. Edits preview on the curves today.

Centre + drift + vibrato

Each note separates its centre, slow drift and vibrato so you correct one without flattening the others — and the micro-jitter that keeps a voice human is never touched.

Audio render ROADMAP

TD-PSOLA rendering of every edit back through the DAW — formant-true shifts, sibilant-safe timing, atomic plosives. The engine the tools are waiting for.

Voice-first DSP ROADMAP
