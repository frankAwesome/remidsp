Doppel

Vocal double alignment — VST3 / AU / Standalone · ARA2 · macOS universal

Capture a guide and its doubles straight from your session (or bind it as ARA and skip the capture entirely), hit ALIGN, and every dub locks to the lead. Doppel splits both takes into phrases, flags anything missing from a dub or extra against the guide, and grades every transient green / gold / red for how tightly it landed.

  • Wordfit-style engine — timing changes hide in the gaps; words play at their natural rate
  • Transient verdicts — per-onset needles and cross-lane tethers show exactly what aligned
  • Phrase detection — backing vocals that skip phrases are sectioned and flagged, not smeared
  • Pitch matching — optional; unison or harmony-aware (a third stays a third), true formant handling
  • Sync points & protected areas — rescue tools when material gets ambiguous
  • ARA2 + capture — one binary; ARA hosts read track audio directly, everyone else captures
