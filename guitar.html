The "Sauce" finalizer

Tone shaping on steroids on a live before/after analyser — an R-Bass sub-harmonic, a threshold-tracked high-end de-esser, transient punch and a dynamic de-harsh.

A tuner that locks

Strobe-accurate to a hundredth of a cent, and it tracks a soft note through hiss — the modern low-volume lock.

Any sample rate
